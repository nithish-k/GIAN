About

Welcome to the 3rd Electronic Class! This class is designed to provide you with an in-depth understanding of electronic systems and devices. Through lectures, lab work, and project-based learning, you'll gain the skills and knowledge needed to become an expert in the field.

Schedule

Here's a general outline of what we'll cover in this course:

  • Week 1: Introduction to Electronics
  • Week 2: Resistors and Capacitors
  • Week 3: Diodes and Transistors
  • Week 4: Op-Amps and Amplifiers
  • Week 5: Digital Logic
  • Week 6: Microcontrollers
  • Week 7: Final Project

Resources

Here are some resources you may find helpful:

  • All About Circuits - a website with tutorials and articles on electronic circuits and systems
  • Digi-Key - an online electronics distributor that offers a wide range of components
  • Arduino - a popular microcontroller platform for prototyping and building electronics projects
